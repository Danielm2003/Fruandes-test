ABOUT US

In Fruandes, with the help of our families of farmers and single mothers we have all our love to make a careful process of fruit selection, slicing, dehydration and packaging in order to deliver to each of our customers the best of each product from our Colombian countryside.

STORY

Our history began in 2002, when Giovanni Porras (Colombian agronomist and son of a mango farmer) shared the vision of economic diversification of Hugo Ciro (Colombian nationalized Canadian who exported coffee to his new country) and together as partners, they established Fruandes (Los Frutos de Andes, Frutas de los Andes).

A year later, in 2003, Fruandes made its first shipment: 180 KG of dried fruit traveled to Canada. The company started employing single mothers to process dried fruit and began to involve more and more small farmers until they became part of a value chain.

In 2007, We encouraged our farmers to completely switch to organic production and helped them in the certification process.
